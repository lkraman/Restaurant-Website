    Our Story Starts With Our Family

    Cafe Americana started with a simple idea. We wanted to serve our own family made-from-scratch, home-cooked meals made with the freshest, quality ingredients we could find.

    Our food symbolizes the heart of North America in combination with a South American flare. Our blended menu is truly about extending the love we have for our family to our extended families...our customers.

    • Freshest ingredients, locally sourced to support our local community
    • Made with quality ingredients. If we wouldn't serve it to our family, we wouldn't serve it to you. We only offer the best!
    • Now offered at two locations located in Kennett Square, Pa and Pike Creek, De. We also offer custom catering for any sized event.
